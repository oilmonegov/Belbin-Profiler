Transfer your points allocation from the seven sections of the Self Perception Inventory to the appropriate boxes below. The pre-printed numbers in the grid refer to the question numbers of each section. For example, if for Section A you scored seven points for question 6 and three points for question 1, you would allocate them in the columns RI and IM respectively.
